Our recommendations:

Sashismi

Sashimi 刺身 describes a delicacy of fish or any other type of raw seafood sliced into bite-sized bits. See in Menu.

Maki 

Maki 巻き is the Japanese word for “roll”. The seaweed (nori) is wrapped around rice and a variety of ingredients. See in Menu.

Uramaki

The meaning of Uramaki 裏巻き is “inside out” roll. The process is reversed, as rice gently wraps around seaweed and other ingredients. See in Menu.

How it started...

After a first-hand experience of the authenticity of flavour in the heart of Japan, Itadori Yūki comes to Romania in the summer of 2002 and establishes in Romania his vision, creating Gojō Sushi Restaurants. It counts as the first step in showing to an unfamiliar public the magnitude of variety contained in the art of sushi-making. 

How it's going...

With over ten years of experience and hard work from our team, we have defined the relationship with our clients by making them the protagonists of the gastronomical experience. After expanding to three more cities, our restaurants have received various awards and national recognition for it's dependable standards of quality. And we don't plan to stop here...  

  • Ecological Rice Farming — We only rely on eco-friendly japanese rice, which is considered ideal for the best sushi. We prepare it in an exclusive way in order to provide a tasty experience for our clients.
  • Sustainable Fishing — Our fish is brought directly from sources of sustainable fishing from professionals and the most trustworthy regions of Norway.
  • Domestic Vegetables — The selection of fruits and vegetables used in our dishes is of national origin and completely supports the local producers.
